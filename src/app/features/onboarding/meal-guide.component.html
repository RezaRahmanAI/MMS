The Quick Input Dashboard

This is your main dashboard for meal entry. Below are the key components you'll use to manage your meals daily.

How to Enter Your Meals

Follow these three simple steps to log your meals for any given day. It takes less than a minute!

calendar_month

1. Select the Date

Use the date selector to pick the day you want to record meals for. You can log for today or upcoming days.

toggle_on

2. Toggle Your Meals

Switch the toggles 'ON' for meals you will have (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner). They are 'ON' by default.

check_circle

3. Click 'Save Meals'

Once you're done, hit the 'Save Meals' button to confirm your entry. A confirmation will appear.

Pro-Tip: Why This is Important
Accurate meal entries ensure the mess manager purchases the right amount of ingredients, reducing food waste and keeping costs down for everyone. It also guarantees your monthly bill is fair and precise.
